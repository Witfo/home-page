Visit Tacoma!

There are so many things to do in and around downtown.

Fun places to go to while visiting Tacoma

The Museum of Glass is a 75,000-square-foot art museum in Tacoma, Washington dedicated to the medium of glass. Local artists constantly put their glass up for everyone to enjoy and purchase.

Cheney Stadium is now the oldest ballpark in the Triple-A West. Tacoma’s baseball home played a role in big league baseball’s earliest days of expansion to the West Coast and has seen some of the game’s biggest names pass through ever since.

A visit to Point Ruston is enjoyable any time of year. The view on the Ruston walk is beautiful and the food around is delicious!
